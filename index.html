Let‚Äôs break this down: Open-source: RStudio believes that it‚Äôs better for everyone if the tools used for research and science are free and open. Publishing system: Quarto is a tool for writing dynamic documents that combine code, output, and text. It can embed output from Python, R, Julia, and Observable.
